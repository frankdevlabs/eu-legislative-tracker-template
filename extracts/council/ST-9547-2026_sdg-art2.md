# Extract — ST 9547/26, Article 2 (Single Digital Gateway, Reg. (EU) 2018/1724) — no operative amendment in this text

> **Source:** Council document **ST 9547/26** (LIMITE), 21 May 2026 — Presidency revised compromise text,
> file 2025/0360 (COD). **Working transcription, not an official text** — consolidated reading. Verify
> against the authoritative document. See [`../../NOTICE`](../../NOTICE).

**This section is absent as operative text in the May compromise.** ST 9547/26 contains **no Omnibus
Article 2** and therefore **no amendment to the Single Digital Gateway Regulation (EU) 2018/1724**. The May
enacting terms run Article 3 (GDPR), 5 (ePrivacy), 6–9 (cyber) and 10–11 (final).

▸ **Apr → May movement.** The SDG Annex II amendment (Omnibus Article 2) appears **only in the April** text
(ST 8261/1/26 REV 1) and is **not carried** in the May compromise. See
`ST-8261-1-26-REV1_sdg-art2.md` and the
SDG instrument page.
