# Extract — ST 9547/26, Article 1 (Data Act, Reg. (EU) 2023/2854) — no operative amendment in this text

> **Source:** Council document **ST 9547/26** (LIMITE), 21 May 2026 — Presidency revised compromise text,
> file 2025/0360 (COD). **Working transcription, not an official text** — consolidated reading. Verify
> against the authoritative document. See [`../../NOTICE`](../../NOTICE).

**This section is absent as operative text in the May compromise.** ST 9547/26 contains **no Omnibus
Article 1** and therefore **no amendment to the Data Act, Regulation (EU) 2023/2854**. The May enacting
terms run **Article 3** (GDPR), **Article 5** (ePrivacy), **Articles 6–9** (cyber) and **Articles 10–11**
(repeals and final) — there is no Article 1 (Data Act) or Article 2 (Single Digital Gateway). The Data Act
is only **cover-listed** among the amended acts.

▸ **Apr → May movement.** The Data Act amendments (Omnibus Article 1 — Chapters VIIa–VIIc / Articles
32a–32z) appear **only in the April** text (ST 8261/1/26 REV 1); they are **not carried** in the May
compromise. See `ST-8261-1-26-REV1_dataact-art1.md` for the substance
and the Data Act instrument page. Note that May still
**repeals** the Data Governance Act, the Free Flow of Non-Personal Data Regulation and the Open Data
Directive (Article 10) — see [final provisions](ST-9547-2026_final-art10-11.md) and the
repealed-instruments page — without the April-style Data Act
consolidation chapters.
